About Yoga

Yoga is not an ancient myth buried in oblivion anymore. Yoga is being increasingly accepted worldwide, as the benefits of yoga are scientifically proven and is practised across the globe for general health and therapeutic purposes. World Health Organisation (WHO) defines complete state of physical, mental and social wellbeing and not merely absence of disease and infirmity. Going by its definition, yoga is ‘The’ best way to attain health as it acts on all levels of being. Dr.Venugopal being a Yoga & Naturopathy consultant and holds a PhD in Yoga, is well versed with over nine different schools of yoga. All our programs are directly crafted through his clinical and research expertise, with a vision to provide essence of the ‘best’ of these different schools of yoga, in a more scientific and evidence-based manner. Yoga, by providing health at all levels of being helps to prevent disease and endocrine disorders even before they come, in sync with the famous adage ‘Prevention is better than cure’. In a nutshell, general yoga helps in preventing countless number of diseases by maintaining a perfect physical body and a calm & relaxed mind.

What’s unique about our program?

‘One hour yoga for stress reduction’ through yoga is not what we are trying to deliver here. We ensure your ‘23 hours’ of peace and wellness following the one hour yoga session, wherein you will be encountering your everyday stressors. There are real stressors which you cannot run away from. It could be your stress out work, relationship, education-related, health issues or anything in-between. Stress reduction is not a onetime fix, and never a onetime fix. That is the reason we don’t believe in delivering one day stress reduction programs.
