About Dr.Venu's

How We Got Here

Dr Venugopal is a doctor, lifestyle consultant & a Diabetes researcher. Over the past 15 years, hundreds of people have experienced a radical shift in their lifestyle and health through his unique and evidence-based lifestyle transformation programs.
He is a scientist working in the field of diabetes reversal & prevention, along with other modern non-communicable diseases (NCDs). His mission is to empower a million people to live their life to the fullest in a healthy and disease-free manner.
Dr.Venugopal has completed his bachelor degree in Yoga & Naturopathy (BNYS), Masters in Diabetes from Edinburgh, UK and a PhD in Yoga/ Diabetes at the world renowned S-VYASA Yoga University. He has over 80 publications in national and international peer reviewed scientific journals and worked on over 6 funded research projects on diabetes and other metabolic disorders.

Decoding-Diabetes

Check Details

Flow of Mind

Decoding diabetes program is one of the specialities of Dr. Venu’s. It is more than just a diabetes education program.
In the modern era of information technology, ‘Google’ could provide all the required knowledge about diabetes and its management. The psycho-social and psychological barriers involved in implementing the same are often overlooked and ‘Decoding diabetes’ is strategically designed taking into account all the key factors impacting the outcomes of diabetes management. All minute detailing are knitted together to make the program more powerful, yet simplified where hands-on training would be provided throughout the course of the program. The biggest success of our program is in making the participants realise that lifestyle transformation is not as difficult as they have previously believed it to be.

What to expect?
  • Uncover & understand diabetes better!

    Learn more than just ABC of Diabetes. From insulin resistance to diabetic neuropathy, learn what’s going on inside your body.

  • Learn yoga as a way of life!

    For people who find no time to perform the 45 minute therapeutic yoga we teach at home, we have a unique solution for you- practise our ‘24 hrs a day yoga’ module. Believe me; it is very much possible and even more effective. Learn how!!!

  • Emotional culturing!

    Diabetes doubles the risk of depression due to its chronic nature. Learn to overcome depression and manage your emotions better, and to shield yourself from negative thoughts and emotions.

  • Translate your prescriptions!

    Your medical prescriptions will not read Greek & Latin anymore. Understand your medicines and what it does, to realise its significance in diabetes management.

  • Diabetes myths & misconceptions!

    Our biggest hit- ‘Diabetes myth busters’! We uncover all the popular myths that are circulating in social media (& more often by your neighbours).

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the philosophy behind your yoga programs?

Our programs are designed to deliver yoga as a comprehensive way of life, addressing physical, mental, and social well-being. Yoga is taught scientifically and evidence-based, ensuring it is practical and accessible for modern lifestyles.

What makes your stress reduction program unique?

We address stress at its roots by redefining how you perceive and manage it. The program includes learning the science of stress, emotional regulation techniques, and practical coping mechanisms. Hands-on training, yogic games, and hobby discovery are integrated to ensure holistic transformation.

What topics are covered in your educational videos?

Our videos simplify complex concepts such as: Evolutionary biology of stress. Psycho-physiology of stress. How the brain perceives and processes stress. The HPA axis and its role in stress response.

How are the workshops conducted?

Workshops are interactive, fun-filled, and hands-on, including: Yogic games for mental agility and team-building. Group activities to foster creativity and relaxation. Personalized exercises tailored to individual needs.

Is your program scientifically validated?

Yes, our programs are rooted in over 15 years of clinical and research expertise, blending ancient yoga principles with modern scientific understanding.

What age groups can join your programs?

Our programs are designed for adults of all age groups. Specific workshops or modules may also cater to younger participants if specified.
